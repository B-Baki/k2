About

Japanese cuisine in the heart of Montreal

Whether you're new to sushi or a seasoned fan, K2 Bistro offers an extensive selection of sushi, ramen, noodles, bento boxes and combo platters in the heart of downtown Montreal.

From fresh hosomaki and nigiri to hearty ramen bowls and satisfying combos, our menu has something for every taste.

  • Freshly prepared sushi and sashimi
  • Hearty ramen bowls and noodle dishes
  • Family-style bento boxes and combo platters
